What is This Tool?

The Tesla Battery Degradation Calculator is a powerful tool that allows Tesla owners to assess the current health of their vehicle's battery. It provides insights into how much battery degradation has occurred over time and helps you maintain optimal performance for your Tesla Model 3, Model S, Model X, or Model Y.

How This Tool Helps

Understanding your Tesla battery's health is crucial for longevity and performance. With this calculator, you can:

  • Estimate how much degradation has occurred.
  • Compare your battery's performance with new benchmarks.
  • Identify potential issues with battery health early on.

How to Use the Tesla Battery Degradation Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your Tesla battery's health:

  1. Input your Tesla's average consumption (in Wh/mi or Wh/km), which you can find on your energy screen.
  2. Enter the projected range that your Tesla is currently reporting.
  3. Enter the current battery percentage level displayed on your Tesla's screen.
  4. Click on the "Calculate Battery Capacity" button to get results for the usable capacity and overall battery health.

How This Tool Works

The calculator uses the average consumption rate, projected range, and current battery percentage to estimate the available battery capacity in kWh.
